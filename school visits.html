As an author, I am happy to visit schools and events to talk about building peace in ourselves, our homes, schools, community, and the world! We don’t only learn about other peacemakers, but about our own power to act as peacemakers. Please contact me if you would like to discuss the possibility of such a visit to your school or event.

PRESENTATIONS

Our presentations are given to childrens and adults of any age and at a wide range of venues. Spreading our message of creating a peaceful world is my utmost joy.

WORKSHOPS

Workshops consist of teaching our younger friends the importance of kindness and the power within us to act as peacemakers.

EXHIBITS

My husband and I travel to hold pop-up shops with our art and we are eager to host at your local exhibition space!
